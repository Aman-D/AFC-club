About Us

The Automotive and Fabrication Club. As the name itself suggests the clubs invokes learning in students by incorporating two major topics which form the building blocks of all the engineering sciences. Here at AFC we believe in "Creating to Learn, Learning to Create" and try to build out of the very basic laws provided by the nature. Constituting of a huge participation from all the years of the UG program we are an ever growing and an ever learning family.

Club Events

Emphasizing on the most natural ways of learning we have always been looking forward to and working towards churning out solutions to the present days problems by looking up towards the very basic building blocks of science by incorporating hydraulics, simple mechanics, combustion systems etc to construct awe inspiring machines.

Projects

Projects for students to take up real life machines and combine electronics, robotics and mechanical systems to bring their ideas to life

Head 1

Do not go by its size. This miniature version of a super car is brutally powerful and obscenely flamboyant and is unburdened by reality. Unflappably stable and crazy fast, it’s capable of amazing performance without feeling like it’s going to snap-spin into a ditch, which is refreshing in any machine which incorporates an IC Engine. With the looks of a naked roadster, it has a 3.48 cc nitro engine and a torque of 6.48 kg-cm and 2.4 GHz, 3 Channel radio control system for steering throttling and breaking. For the ultimate, the throttle car is a beast born and bred in the arenas of IIITDMJ and claims a top speed of 40 mph in our testing

  • Engine: 3.48 cc
  • Chassis Metal: Mild Steel Plate.
  • Wheelbase: 322mm (12.7 in)
  • Control System: 2.4 GHz 3 Channel Radio Control System
  • Suspension: C-Hub Suspension
